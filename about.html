About NUST H12 Navigator

Your ultimate guide to navigating the NUST H12 campus with ease.

Our Mission

The NUST H12 Navigator was created to simplify campus navigation for students, staff, and visitors. With detailed maps, directions, and useful information, our goal is to make your experience at NUST H12 seamless and enjoyable.

Key Features

  • Interactive Maps: Find your way around the campus with pinpoint accuracy.
  • Searchable Locations: Quickly locate buildings, cafes, and other points of interest.
  • Event Updates: Stay informed about upcoming events on campus.
  • Campus Cafes: Discover the best spots for a coffee or meal.

Meet the Team

The NUST H12 Navigator was developed by a group of passionate Data Science students from NUST. We combined our love for technology and innovation to create a tool that enhances the campus experience.

Special thanks to the guidance provided by our professors and peers!
